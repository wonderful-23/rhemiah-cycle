Explore Your Cycle Phases

Understanding each phase helps you notice patterns in energy, mood, and flow.

Menstrual Phase 🌙

Time for rest and reflection. Your body sheds the uterine lining, and energy is often lower. Gentle self-care is key.

Follicular Phase 🌱

Energy rises as follicles mature. Creativity and focus increase. Perfect time for learning, planning, and new projects.

Ovulation Phase 🌸

Peak fertility and social energy. Confidence and focus are high. A great time for collaboration and communication.

Luteal Phase 🌺

The body prepares for the next cycle. Energy may fluctuate, and self-care routines support comfort and balance.
